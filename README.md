# Hi, I'm Alisa

Researcher and urban planner working at the intersection of urban space 
and sociology. My research interests include mobility and last-mile logistics, 
spatial inequality, national and cultural identity, and spatial memory — 
approached through data analysis and fieldwork.

## Education

- BA Architecture
- MA Urban Development, HSE Moscow
- MA Sociology with Distinction, University of Manchester

## Experience

- World Bank consultancy on urban development in Uzbekistan
- Leadership roles at Tashkent City Administration
- Moscow Urban Forum and urban development institutes

## Currently

Learning R and computational methods in social science. Building a public 
portfolio of open data projects on urban logistics, spatial inequality, 
and the spatial expression of identity.

## Research interests

- Platform economies and urban space
- Last-mile logistics and retail justice
- Spatial inequality and access
- Mobility and everyday urban life
- Urban memory and cultural identity
